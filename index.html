ABOUT

Hi, I'm a geoscientist interested in volcanology and geophysics, currently focusing on magmatic reservoir dynamics and magma physics and acoustics.

I use Computational Fluid Dynamics and Discrete Element Method (CFD-DEM) numerical modeling to study the dynamics of magmatic reservoirs and understand what physical processes may generate an eruption. I also use analytical appraoches to study the physics and acoustics of magmas.

During my PhD I focused on determining the physical processes

I'm currently a posdoctoral research associate at department of Earth and Space Sciences at the university of Washington, Seattle, working in the lab of Prof. George bergantz.
